What The Event Covers

Short on clutter. Heavy on atmosphere, control, and live shooting.

This event page stays intentionally lean. The workshop itself is not a lecture dump or a diagram archive. It is a guided studio experience built around disciplined light placement, tasteful figure work, and selective image release.

  • Five signature lighting looks Chiaroscuro, side and rim light, raking light, low-key highlights, and controlled multi-light refinements.
  • Live demonstration and guided shooting You will watch the setups unfold, then rotate into photographing the model inside the same controlled lighting environment.
  • Private post-event gallery credit Selected tasteful frames may be released later in a private gallery with photographer credit. Unreleased images remain restricted.

Before You Arrive

Participation rules are part of the event, not an afterthought.

This workshop involves photographing a nude model. To protect the model, the studio environment, and every attendee, the event runs under strict handling rules from arrival through final image release.

Signed agreement required

Every participant must complete the confidentiality agreement before attending. No signed agreement means no entry.

No phones or social capture

Phones are surrendered on arrival. No behind-the-scenes posting, no stories, and no unauthorized social media content.

Controlled file release

SD cards are surrendered after shooting for file transfer. Participants do not leave with their full captures from the session.

Only approved selects may be shared later

Released images chosen after review may be credited and used in portfolios or social media. Everything else remains unreleased.
